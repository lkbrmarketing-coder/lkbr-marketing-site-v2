About Us

My Story

Hi, I'm Lukas Karlsson.

I founded LKBR Marketing LLC in Scottsdale, Arizona after watching too many great local businesses become invisible online. A plumber with 20 years of experience losing jobs to a competitor with a basic website. A restaurant with incredible food that nobody could find on Google. That gap between a great business and a great online presence is exactly what LKBR Marketing exists to close.

I'm not a big agency with $10,000 minimums and account managers you never actually talk to. I'm one person, fully dedicated to your project, using the latest AI tools to build better websites faster and more affordably than anyone else in the market. You get direct access to me from the first conversation to the final launch.

Every website I build is custom — not a template, not a drag-and-drop builder. Clean code that loads fast, looks great on every device, and is structured so Google can find it. When we're done, you own everything. No subscriptions, no lock-in, no dependency on LKBR Marketing.

I also help businesses stay visible after the website launches through content creation — social media graphics, promotional materials, and brand assets that keep your business active and in front of customers online.

Lukas Karlsson — Founder & Creative Director, LKBR Marketing LLC · Scottsdale, Arizona
